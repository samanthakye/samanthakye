How Pinterest Was Made

What I Learned

  • At first they just used Python and MySQL.
  • They added caching with Memcache and Redis to make the site faster.
  • As more people joined, they used more servers and split their databases.
  • Now it’s way bigger: they have microservices, data pipelines with Kafka, and they keep everything in the cloud (AWS).
  • The front end (what we see) is built with React, which makes the pin grid smooth.
